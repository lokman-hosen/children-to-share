Safety First

We prioritize the safety and privacy of all children on our platform

Our Safety Features

  • Guardian verification for all under-18 users
  • No direct child-to-child messaging
  • Location privacy controls
  • Content moderation for all posts
  • Guardian-to-guardian communication only
  • Secure data encryption
